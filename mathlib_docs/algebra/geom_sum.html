Sum of the finite geometric series $∑_{i=0}^{n-1} x^i$.

geom_series_def

∀ {α : Type u} [_inst_1 : semiring α] (x : α) (n : ℕ), geom_series x n = finset.sum (finset.range n) (λ (i : ℕ), x ^ i)
