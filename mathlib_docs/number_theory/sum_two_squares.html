Sums of two squares

Proof of Fermat's theorem on the sum of two squares. Every prime congruent to 1 mod 4 is the sum of two squares

nat.prime.sum_two_squares {p : ℕ} (hp : nat.prime p) (hp1 : p % 4 = 1) :
∃ (a b : ℕ), a ^ 2 + b ^ 2 = p
